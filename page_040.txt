In some factories cutting machines are used which will
cut into bars a considerable number of slabs at one time.
This machine consists of strong wooden framework with
wrought-iron fittings, and a series of steel wires fixed at
equal distances. Although this machine is capable of
cutting a great number of bars by a single movement, the
wires are very liable to break, .and this frequently causes
delay while the broken wires are being replaced. The
