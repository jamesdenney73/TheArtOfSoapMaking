For bleaching raw silk, white olive-oil soap is used on

Oleic acid, saponified by potash ley, is a very suitable
fatty material for making soft soap. The first potash ley
should have a strength equal to about 20° B., and the soap
may be finished with a stronger ley—from 25° to 28°,
