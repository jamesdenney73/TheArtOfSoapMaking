subside. The ley is then drawn off and the lime washed
several times with fresh water, the last runnings being
used instead of water in future operations.

Caustic potash, for employment in the manufacture of
soft soaps, is prepared in the same way as caustic soda,
except that eighty parts of lime to each hundred of potash
must be used.
